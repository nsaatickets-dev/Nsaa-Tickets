About Nsaa

A ticketing platform Ghana can trust.

Built for serious organizers and everyday buyers who want clear prices, real inventory, and a smooth door experience - whether that's a 200-seat church conference in Kumasi or a 5,000-person Independence Square show.

Not just concerts. Not just nightlife.

Nsaa covers the full rhythm of Ghanaian events: conferences, sports, weddings, worship nights, comedy, theatre, workshops, concerts, and curated nightlife. The platform chrome stays consistent while each event keeps its own voice.

No late surprises

Ticket subtotal, service fee, and total are itemized before payment starts - the number at checkout is the number that leaves your Mobile Money balance.

Accounts are optional

Pay with a name and a phone number, then sign in later if you want your tickets in one place.

Every ticket is signed

Each paid ticket gets a signed code, validated once at the door. Reused or forwarded codes are rejected on the spot.

Your seat waits ten minutes

Checkout reserves inventory the moment you start and releases it automatically if the Mobile Money prompt goes unanswered.

A premium frame for every organizer.

Nsaa is designed to serve church teams, wedding planners, sports houses, conference producers, comedians, theatre companies, nightlife operators, and major live-event brands with the same level of care.
