Accounts

An optional account (via our authentication provider, Clerk) simply links your past orders together for convenience. If you do create one, you're responsible for keeping your login credentials secure and your account details accurate.

Organizer responsibilities

Organizers are responsible for the accuracy of their event listings (venue, date, pricing, description), honoring every ticket sold through Nsaa, and complying with applicable law for the events they run. Organizers choose a pricing tier at event creation (Free, Essential, or Pro, or a Custom rate arranged directly with us) that sets Nsaa's service fee percentage on paid tickets; this fee is separate from, and unaffected by, whatever a payment processor charges. Payouts of ticket revenue are released to the organizer's registered Mobile Money number after an event's scheduled end date has passed, not before - this protects both organizers and buyers if an event needs to be cancelled or disputed before revenue is paid out.

See the Acceptable Use Policy for the full list of what's not allowed - in short: no reselling or transferring tickets, no automated/bot purchasing, no fraudulent payment details, no forging or tampering with QR tickets, and no listings that are illegal or misrepresent the organizer.

Payments

Payment collection (Mobile Money and card) is handled by our payment processor, Moolre. Nsaa never collects or stores your Mobile Money PIN or full card number. Starting checkout holds your selected tickets for 10 minutes while you complete payment; if payment isn't confirmed in that window, the hold expires automatically and the tickets return to general availability.
